Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or interact with our call-based booking services for Delta Airlines. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data when you visit our site or call our support number:

  • Full name and contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Call metadata such as call duration, time, and call recordings (for quality assurance)
  • Browser data and cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Assist with your flight booking over the phone
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Call center partners assisting with bookings
  • Analytics and tracking services (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities if required

4. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies, pixels, and tracking scripts to understand website traffic and ad campaign effectiveness. These tools do not reveal personal identity but may track browser/device usage.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content. We encourage you to read their policies.

6. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method is 100% secure. Please use caution when providing sensitive information online.

7. Your Choices

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Opt-out of email or SMS communications
  • Inquire about how your data is used

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted here with a revised effective date.
